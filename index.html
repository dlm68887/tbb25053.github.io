Formation of Shop Brunch Babe

Growing up with my sisters Nicole (23) and Macy(17), we were always sharing clothes. Nicole and I are only 2 years apart in age so we tended to share clothes more frequently than Macy and I. Through all of the childhood disputes on whose shirt was whose and miscommunication on borrowing things, fashion was still something that the three of us all bonded over. Nicole attended college in Atlanta so she could get a Bachelor’s Degree while performing as a Falcons Cheerleader, and I decided to go to UGA to enjoy the perks of a big school and SEC Saturday’s. Since Macy, is younger than Nicole and me, it was difficult for her to be hands on with the upstart of our online boutique, but Macy still contributes by picking out items and marketing them on social media. Even with the distance and the age difference, Shop Brunch Babe has been something to continuously bring us together. p>
